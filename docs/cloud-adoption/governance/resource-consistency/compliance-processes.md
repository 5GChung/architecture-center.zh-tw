## <a name="planning-review-and-reporting-processes"></a><span data-ttu-id="00e13-107">規劃、檢閱及報告流程</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-107">Planning, review, and reporting processes</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-108">雲端平台提供一系列的管理工具和功能，您可以用來組織、佈建、調整及縮短停機時間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-108">Cloud platforms provide an array of management tools and features that you can use to organize, provision, scale, and minimize downtime.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-109">若要以降低潛在風險的方式使用這些工具，以有效地建構及操作您的雲端部署，您需要完善的程序和原則，並且要與 IT 作業小組及業務專案關係人密切合作。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-109">Using these tools to effectively structure and operate your cloud deployments in ways that mitigate potential risks requires well thought out processes and policies in addition to close cooperation with IT Operations teams and business stakeholders.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-110">以下是一組資源一致性專業領域經常會牽涉到的範例流程。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-110">The following is a set of example processes commonly involved in the Resource Consistency discipline.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-111">當您規劃可讓您根據業務變更和開發及 IT 小組 (賦有將指引付諸行動的工作) 意見反應來繼續更資源一致性原則的流程時，使用這些範例作為起點。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-111">Use these examples as a starting point when planning the processes that will allow you to continue to update Resource Consistency policy based on business change and feedback from the development and IT teams tasked with turning guidance into action.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-112">**初始風險評估及規劃**：在您初次採用資源一致性專業領域時，識別您的核心業務風險和與作業及 IT 管理相關的承受度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-112">**Initial risk assessment and planning**: As part of your initial adoption of the Resource Consistency discipline, identify your core business risks and tolerances related to operations and IT management.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-113">使用此資訊與您的 IT 小組成員及工作負載擁有者討論特定技術風險，以開發一組用來降低這些風險的基本資源一致性原則，進而建立初始治理策略。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-113">Use this information to discuss specific technical risks with members of your IT teams and workload owners to develop a baseline set of Resource Consistency policies designed to mitigate these risks, establishing your initial governance strategy.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-114">**部署規劃**：在部署任何資產之前，執行檢閱動作來找出任何新的操作風險。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-114">**Deployment planning**: Prior to deployment of any asset, perform a review to identify any new operational risks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-115">建立資源需求和預期需求模式，並找出延展性需求和使用方式最佳化的潛在機會。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-115">Establish resource requirements and expected demand patterns, and identify scalability needs and potential usage optimization opportunities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-116">也請確定備份和復原計劃已就緒。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-116">Also ensure backup and recovery plans are in place.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-117">**部署測試**：在部署時，雲端治理小組 (與您的雲端作業小組合作) 負責檢閱部署以驗證資源一致性原則的合規性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-117">**Deployment testing**: As part of deployment, the Cloud Governance team, in cooperation with your cloud operations teams, will be responsible for reviewing the deployment to validate Resource Consistency policy compliance.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-118">**年度規劃**：每年執行高階的資源一致性策略檢閱。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-118">**Annual planning**: On an annual basis, perform a high-level review of Resource Consistency strategy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-119">探索公司未來的擴充計劃或優先順序，以及更新雲端採用策略，以找出可能提高的風險或其他新的資源一致性需求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-119">Explore future corporate expansion plans or priorities and update cloud adoption strategies to identify potential risk increase or other emerging Resource Consistency needs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-120">同時利用這個機會檢閱最新的雲端資源一致性最佳做法，並且將其整合至您的原則和檢閱流程。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-120">Also use this time to review the latest best practices for cloud Resource Consistency and integrate these into your policies and review processes.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-121">**每季檢閱與規劃**：每季檢閱作業資料和事件報告，以識別資源一致性原則中任何必要的變更。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-121">**Quarterly review and planning**: On a quarterly basis perform a review of operational data and incident reports to identify any changes required in Resource Consistency policy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-122">在執行此程序時，檢閱資源使用方式和效能中的變更，以識別需要增加或減少資源配置的資產，以及識別任何應淘汰的工作負載或資產。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-122">As part of this process, review changes in resource usage and performance to identify assets that require increases or decreases in resource allocation, and identify any workloads or assets that are candidates for retirement.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-123">此規劃流程也是評估您雲端治理小組目前成員資格的好時機，以了解最新或演進原則的相關差距，以及資源一致性專業領域的相關風險。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-123">This planning process is also a good time to evaluate the current membership of your Cloud Governance team for knowledge gaps related to new or evolving policy and risks related to Resource Consistency as a discipline.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-124">邀請相關 IT 人員來參與檢閱及規劃，無論是作為小組的暫時技術顧問或永久成員。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-124">Invite relevant IT staff to participate in reviews and planning as either temporary technical advisors or permanent members of your team.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-125">**教育與訓練**：每兩個月提供訓練課程，以確定 IT 人員和開發人員跟上最新的資源一致性原則需求和指導。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-125">**Education and Training**: On a bi-monthly basis, offer training sessions to make sure IT staff and developers are up-to-date on the latest Resource Consistency policy requirements and guidance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-126">在此流程中檢閱及更新任何文件或其他訓練資產，以確保這些項目與最新的公司原則聲明同步。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-126">As part of this process review and update any documentation or other training assets to ensure they are in sync with the latest corporate policy statements.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-127">**每月稽核和報告檢閱**：每月對所有雲端部署執行稽核，以確保這些部署與資源一致性原則持續保持一致。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-127">**Monthly audit and reporting reviews**: On a monthly basis, perform an audit on all cloud deployments to assure their continued alignment with Resource Consistency policy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-128">檢閱 IT 人員的相關活動，並識別任何未在現行監視和強制程序中處理的合規性問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-128">Review related activities with IT staff and identify any compliance issues not already handled as part of the ongoing monitoring and enforcement process.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-129">此檢閱的結果會向雲端策略小組和每個雲端採用小組報告，以告知整體的效能及原則遵循狀況。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-129">The result of this review is a report for the Cloud Strategy team and each cloud adoption team to communicate overall performance and adherence to policy.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-130">報告也會儲存以便用於稽核和法律用途。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-130">The report is also stored for auditing and legal purposes.</span></span>

## <a name="ongoing-monitoring-processes"></a><span data-ttu-id="00e13-131">持續監視流程</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-131">Ongoing monitoring processes</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-132">根據您雲端基礎結構目前和過去狀態的可見度，判斷您的資源一致性治理策略是否成功。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-132">Determining if your Resource Consistency governance strategy is successful depends on visibility into the current and past state of your cloud infrastructure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-133">如果無法分析您雲端環境健康情況和活動的相關計量和資料，您就無法識別風險的變更或者偵測是否違反風險承受度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-133">Without the ability to analyze the relevant metrics and data of your cloud environment's health and activity, you cannot identify changes in your risks or detect violations of your risk tolerances.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-134">上面所討論的現行治理程序需要品質資料，才可確定能修改原則以最佳化雲端資源使用情況，並改善雲端託管工作負載的整體效能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-134">The ongoing governance processes discussed above require quality data to ensure policy can be modified to optimize your cloud resource usage and improve overall performance of cloud-hosted workloads.</span></span>

## <a name="violation-triggers-and-enforcement-actions"></a><span data-ttu-id="00e13-137">違規觸發程序及強制執行動作</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-137">Violation triggers and enforcement actions</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="00e13-138">由於資源一致性原則的合規性可能會導致重大的服務中斷或重大超支風險，因此雲端治理小組應要能察覺不符合規範的事件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-138">Because Resource Consistency policy compliance can lead to critical service disruption or significant cost overruns risks, the Cloud Governance team should have visibility into noncompliance incidents.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00e13-139">請確定 IT 人員有清楚的呈報路徑，可將這些問題回報給最適合識別並確認原則問題是否已緩和的治理小組成員。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00e13-139">Ensure IT staff have clear escalation paths for reporting these issues to the governance team members best suited to identify and verify that policy issues are mitigated.</span></span>

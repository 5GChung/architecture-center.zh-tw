<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-173">**遺失影響**：用來判斷風險影響的問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-173">**Loss Impact**: Questions to determine the impact of a risk.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-174">這些問題可能較不容易 (有時甚至不可能) 找出答案。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-174">These questions can be difficult (sometimes impossible) to answer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-175">能將影響量化當然是最好的，但有時候光是對話本身便足以了解承受度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-175">Quantifying the impact is best, but sometimes the conversation alone is enough to understand tolerance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-176">範圍也是可以接受的，特別是當它們包含能決定那些範圍的假設。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-176">Ranges are also acceptable, especially if they include assumptions that determined those ranges.</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-177">此風險是否違反第三方合規性需求？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-177">Does this risk violate third-party compliance requirements?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-178">此風險是否違反內部公司原則？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-178">Does this risk violate internal corporate policies?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-179">此風險是否會降低客戶或市場佔有率？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-179">Could this risk cost customers or market share?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-180">如果是，此影響是否可以量化？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-180">If so, can this impact be quantified?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-181">此風險是否會產生負面的客戶體驗？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-181">Could this risk create negative customer experiences?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-182">那些體驗是否會影響銷售或營收的實現？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-182">Are those experiences likely to impact sales or revenue realization?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-183">此風險是否會產生新的法律責任？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-183">Could this risk create new legal liability?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-184">如果是，這些類型的案例是否有損害賠償的先例？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-184">If so, is there a precedence for damage awards in these types of cases?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-185">此風險是否會停止商務營運？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-185">Could this risk stop business operations?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-186">如果是，營運中斷時間的長度為何？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-186">If so, how long would operations be down?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-187">此風險是否會減緩商務營運？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-187">Could this risk slow business operations?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-188">如果是，減緩程度與時間長度為何？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-188">If so, how slow & how long?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-189">在此階段的轉換中，此風險是否只會單次發生，還是會重複發生？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-189">At this stage in the transformation, is this a one-off risk or will it repeat?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-190">風險的發生頻率是否會隨著轉換進度提升或降低？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-190">Does the risk increase or decrease in frequency as the transformation progresses?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-191">風險的發生機率是否會隨著時間提升或降低？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-191">Does the risk increase or decrease in probability over time?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-192">風險本身是否有時間性？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-192">Is the risk time sensitive in nature?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-193">如果不加以處理，風險會消失還是會加劇？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-193">Will the risk pass or get worse, if not addressed?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-194">這些基本問題將能引發更多其他討論。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-194">These basic questions will lead to many more.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-195">在進行健康的對話之後，建議將相關的風險記錄下來，並在可行的情況下加以量化。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-195">After exploring a healthy dialogue, it is suggested that the relevant risks be recorded and when possible quantified.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-196">**風險降低成本**：用來判斷減輕或移除風險之成本的問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-196">**Mitigation costs**: Questions to determine the cost of mitigating or removing the risk.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-197">這些問題可能相當直接，特別是在存在某個範圍的情況下。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-197">These questions can be fairly direct, especially when represented in a range.</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-198">是否有清楚的解決方案？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-198">Is there a clear solution?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-199">其成本為何？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-199">What does it cost?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-200">是否有解決或減輕此風險的選項？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-200">Are there options to solving or mitigating this risk?</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-201">那些解決方案的成本範圍為何？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-201">What is the range of costs for those solutions?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-202">如果要選出最佳且明確的解決方案，商務團隊需要提供什麼？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-202">What is needed from the business to select the best, clear solution?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-203">如果要驗證成本，商務團隊要提供什麼？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-203">What is needed from the business to validate costs?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-204">減輕此風險的解決方案還能提供什麼其他優點？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-204">What other benefits can come from the solution that would mitigate this risk?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-205">這些問題會過度簡化減輕風險所需的技術性解決方案。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-205">These questions over simplify the technical solutions needed to mitigate risks.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-206">不過，這些問題能夠以商務團隊可迅速整合成決策程序的方式來溝通那些解決方案。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-206">However, these questions communicate those solutions in ways the business can quickly integrate into a decision process.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-207">**遺失的機率**：用來判斷風險變成現實之機率的問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-207">**Probability of Loss**: Questions to determine how likely it is that the risk will become a reality.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-208">這是最難以量化的領域。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-208">This is the most difficult area to quantify.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-209">我們改為建議雲端治理團隊根據支援的資料建立類別以溝通機率。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-209">Instead it is suggested that the Cloud Governance team create categories for communicating probability, based on the supporting data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-210">下列問題可以協助建立對團隊有意義的類別。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-210">The following questions can help create categories that are meaningful to the team.</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-211">是否有任何針對此風險變成現實的機率所做的研究？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-211">Has any research been done regarding the likelihood of this risk being realized?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-212">廠商是否能針對產生影響的機率提供參考或統計資料？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-212">Can the vendor provide references or statistics on the likelihood of impact?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-213">在其他相關領域或垂直結構中，是否有任何其他公司曾受到此風險的影響？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-213">Are there other companies in the relevant sector or vertical that have been hit by this risk?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-214">再退一步想，就一般而言，是否有任何其他公司曾受到此風險的影響？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-214">Look further, are there other companies in general that have been hit by this risk?</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-215">此風險是否是因為此公司未妥善處理某個領域所導致？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-215">Is this risk unique to something this company has done poorly?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-216">在回答這些問題，以及其他雲端治理團隊所提出的問題之後，機率的群組很有可能已經浮現。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-216">After answering these questions & more questions as determined by the Cloud Governance team, groupings of probability will likely emerge.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-217">下列是幾個可協助開始的群組範例：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-217">The following are a few grouping samples to help get started:</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-218">無指示：沒有完成足夠的研究以判斷機率</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-218">No indication: Not enough research has been completed to determine probability</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-219">低度風險：目前的研究顯示該風險不太可能實現</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-219">Low Risk: Current research suggests the risk is not likely to be realized</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-220">未來風險：目前的機率是「低風險」。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-220">Future risk: The current probability is Low Risk.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-221">不過，持續的採用將會觸發新的分析</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-221">However, continued adoption would trigger a fresh analysis</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-222">中度風險：風險很有可能會影響企業</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-222">Medium Risk: It's likely that the risk will impact the business</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-223">高度風險：隨著時間，企業將越來越無法避免來自此風險的影響</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-223">High Risk: Overtime, it is increasing less likely that the business will avoid impact from this risk</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-224">衰退風險：風險為「中度」到「高度」。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-224">Declining Risk: The risk is Medium to High.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-225">不過，由 IT 或商務所採取的行動正在降低產生影響的機會。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-225">However, actions in IT or business are reducing the likelihood of an impact.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-226">**判斷承受度：**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-226">**Determining Tolerance:**</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="79b7a-227">上述的三個問題集合應該能提供足夠的資料以判斷初始的承受度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-227">The three question sets above should fuel enough data to determine initial tolerances.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-228">當風險和機率皆較低，且風險降低的成本較高時，商務團隊較不容易在補救措施上投入資源。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-228">When risk & probability are low, and mitigation cost is high, the business is unlikely to invest in remediation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="79b7a-229">當風險和機率皆較高時，商務團隊較可能會考慮投入資源，前提是其成本不能超過潛在風險。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="79b7a-229">When risk & probability are high, the business is likely to consider an investment, as long as the costs don't exceed the potential risks.</span></span>
